# ISORYN

> Trade the Future on Continuous Curves.

Isoryn is a next-generation on-chain prediction derivatives protocol built on Solana. Instead of limiting traders to binary **YES/NO** outcomes, Isoryn introduces continuous prediction curves, allowing users to express nuanced market expectations with greater precision, flexibility, and capital efficiency.

Designed for sophisticated traders, liquidity providers, and DeFi builders, Isoryn transforms prediction markets into programmable financial derivatives.

---

## 🚀 What is Isoryn?

Traditional prediction markets force traders into binary positions:

* YES
* NO

Real-world outcomes are rarely binary.

Isoryn enables users to trade probability distributions through continuous pricing curves, creating richer market dynamics and more accurate price discovery. Continuous prediction markets and AMM-based derivatives have emerged as an alternative to winner-take-all binary markets by allowing graded outcomes and smoother pricing mechanisms.
